Out Of: Science-Driven Performance

Out Of is an Italian brand that enhances outdoor performance through cutting-edge technology and science. Founded by three siblings with expertise in physics, industrial, and graphic design, Out Of has grown from a small startup to a leader in innovation. Their product line includes advanced technologies like IRID electronic lenses, designed to push boundaries and deliver unparalleled outdoor experiences. A certified B Corp since 2024, Out Of is committed to responsible business and continuous improvement, supported by a passionate community of enthusiasts and notable investors like Ferrari and Azimut.

Plastinax Eyewear is proud to be Out Of's exclusive distributor in the Indian Ocean bringing these high-performance, science-driven products to outdoor enthusiasts.
